ABOUT US

Friendly Integrated Development Initiative in Poverty Alleviation (FIDIPA) is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) registered in Kenya in 2007. As a grassroots, faith-based NGO, FIDIPA fosters unity, participation, and cooperation with marginalized urban and rural populations.
